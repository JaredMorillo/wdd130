1. Lemon with Watermelon and Melon

If you are thirsty and had troubles with your heart, this drink can help you a lot. This drink help you to increase your metabolism and made your heart more stronger.

  • INGREDIENTS
  • 1 Liter of very cold sparkling water
  • 1 Dash of lemon juice
  • 1 Piece of melon
  • 1 Piece of Watermelon
  • White sugar to taste
  • Mint
  • Ice

2. Watermelon Lemonade with Ginger

Take advantage of the fruits with more water to prepare the most refreshing lemonades. Watermelon is the best example because almost 95% of its weight is water. In this case, we are going to sweeten it with a syrup and flavor it with ginger and cinnamon. It is perfect to quench your thirst but you can also have it as a dessert.

  • INGREDIENTS
  • 400 g of watermelon
  • 1 Unit of Lima
  • 3 Tbsp of white sugar
  • 1 Pieces of fresh ginger grated
  • 1 Cc of ground Cinnamon
  • Herb mix for garnish

3. Red Fruit Lemonade

Many of the red fruits that we know are extremely rich in antioxidants, so they will help to cleanse our body of those things that do not go well, coupled with this they are ideal for preventing cardiovascular problems. If you suffer from constipation problems, red fruits could become a great ally,since due to its high fiber content, it is really friendly with our digestive system.

  • INGREDIENTS
  • 100 g Raspberries
  • 100 g Strawberries
  • 100 g Of cranberries
  • 1 Unit of lemon juice
